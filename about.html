WELCOME TO MY PORTFOLIO

Hello! I'm Omer Bin Dawood, a passionate Data Scientist and MERN Stack developer from Sheikhupura, Punjab, Pakistan based in Islamabad Pakistan. With a strong background in Data Science and Full Stack Development, I thrive on bringing innovative ideas to life through clean and efficient code.
